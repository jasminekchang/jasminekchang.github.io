Introduction

Bark by the Park

Bark by the Park is a pet shop catered towards dogs. It provides a variety of dog food, supplies, grooming services, and training services for dogs of all age and size.

Target audience

The people who use this site are either those that are dog owners looking for dog specialization in products and services or anybody that needs dog advice and have questions that need answering.

Their primary task and goal is to get supplies or services for their dogs. Bark by the Park is a center for all dog-related things, and customers come in and expect to get their needs fulfilled.

Services

Grooming

Here at Bark by the Park, we have academy certified Dog Stylists that have had extensive training from bathing and trimming to styling all kinds of dogs, big and small! We currently offer bath and haircut services. To book your appointment or check pricing with us, call (850)672-9870.

[Dogs at Bark by the Park Grooming Salon.]

Training

At Bark by the Park, our mission is to provide all dogs and owners with proper care, and this includes basic training to strengthen a healthier dog-owner bond! Training is more than "sit", "come", and "stay". it's about trust and continued learning. Our services include group-based and individual sessions for all dogs of any breed and size. We offer a free one-week trial to any new customers before any subscription for exclusive classes. To book a free trial for our training camp, please call (850)672-9871.

[Dogs at Bark by the Park Training Camp.]

About Us

Bark by the Park is a locally owned and operated dog food, supply, and services store. Our mission is to provide the best assistance and care for owners and their pups. We sell all kinds of dog-related products, parterning with over 20 different brands to bring you the best quality that you deserve. We started off as a small family owned shop based in San Luis Obispo. After years of hard work, dedication, and teamwork, we have transformed our small shop into a local business, becoming a local favorite for all dog owners.

If you have any questions about us or about dogs, we'd love to answer them! Come in and check out our store or call for any information.
